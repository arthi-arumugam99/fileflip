Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FileFlip

How much does FileFlip cost?

FileFlip is a one-time purchase of $12.99. No subscriptions, no monthly fees, no hidden costs. Pay once and own it forever, including all future updates.

Does FileFlip upload my files to the cloud?

No. FileFlip works completely offline. Your files are processed locally on your computer and never uploaded to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security for your sensitive documents.

What file formats are supported?

FileFlip supports 70+ formats including images (PNG, JPG, WebP, HEIC, AVIF, GIF, BMP, TIFF, SVG), documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT, Markdown, HTML, RTF), audio (MP3, WAV, FLAC, AAC, OGG), and video (MP4, MKV, WebM, AVI, MOV).

How do I convert HEIC to JPG?

Simply drag and drop your HEIC files into FileFlip, select JPG as the output format, and click Convert. The conversion happens instantly on your device without uploading anything.

Can I convert multiple files at once?

Yes! FileFlip supports batch conversion. You can drag and drop hundreds of files at once and convert them all with a single click. Perfect for processing entire photo libraries or document collections.

Do I need FFmpeg for audio/video conversion?

For audio and video conversion, FileFlip uses FFmpeg which you'll need to install separately. Image and document conversions work without any additional software.

Is FileFlip open source?

Yes, FileFlip is completely open source under the MIT license. You can view, modify, and contribute to the source code on GitHub. We believe in transparency and community-driven development.

What operating systems are supported?

FileFlip runs on Windows 10/11, macOS 10.13 or later (both Intel and Apple Silicon), and Linux (Ubuntu, Fedora, Arch, and other distributions via AppImage, DEB, or RPM).
