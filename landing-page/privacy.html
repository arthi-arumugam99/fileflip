Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 30, 2025 | Last Updated: January 30, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how FileFlip ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our desktop file conversion application ("FileFlip" or the "Software"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Key Point: FileFlip is designed to be 100% offline. We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any of your personal data or files.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

FileFlip operates entirely on your local device without any network connectivity requirements. Specifically:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information through the Software.
  • No Account Required: FileFlip does not require you to create an account or sign in.
  • No Usage Data: We do not collect analytics, usage statistics, crash reports, or telemetry data.
  • No File Data: Your files are never uploaded, transmitted, or accessible to us. All processing occurs locally on your device.
  • No Cookies or Tracking: The Software does not use cookies, web beacons, pixels, or any tracking technologies.
  • No Third-Party Analytics: We do not integrate Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, or any other analytics services.

2. How Your Files Are Processed

FileFlip processes all files locally on your device:

  • Files you select for conversion remain on your computer at all times.
  • No internet connection is required for any conversion operation.
  • Converted files are saved to your specified output location on your local storage.
  • Original files are never modified unless you explicitly choose to overwrite them.
  • Temporary files created during conversion are stored locally and deleted after the operation completes.
  • We have no technical ability to access, view, or retrieve your files.

3. Information Collected by App Stores

When you purchase or download FileFlip through an app store (Microsoft Store, Mac App Store, or Snap Store), those platforms may collect information according to their own privacy policies. This may include:

  • Purchase and transaction information
  • Account information associated with your app store account
  • Device information for licensing purposes

Important: FileFlip does not receive, access, or store any personal information from these transactions. We only receive confirmation that a valid license exists. Please review the privacy policies of the respective app stores for more information:

  • Microsoft Privacy Statement
  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Canonical/Snap Store Privacy Policy

4. Support Communications

  • This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and provide technical support.

  • You may request deletion of your support communications at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

FileFlip does not integrate with or transmit data to any third-party services, advertising networks, or data brokers. The Software:

  • Does not contain advertisements
  • Does not include social media integrations
  • Does not connect to external APIs for functionality
  • Does not share data with analytics providers

FFmpeg: For audio and video conversion, FileFlip may utilize FFmpeg, an open-source multimedia framework that runs locally on your device. FFmpeg does not collect or transmit any data.

6. Data Security

Because FileFlip does not collect or store your data on external servers, your information security depends on your own device security measures. We recommend:

  • Keeping your operating system and software up to date
  • Using strong passwords and encryption on your device
  • Following your organization's security policies if applicable

FileFlip is designed with privacy as a core principle - we cannot lose, breach, or misuse data we never collect.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

FileFlip does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. Because we do not collect any data:

  • We do not knowingly collect information from children
  • There is no data to delete or manage
  • The Software is safe for users of all ages
  • Parental consent is not required as no data collection occurs

8. International Users and Data Transfers

FileFlip is available worldwide. Because all processing occurs locally on your device and we do not collect or transfer any personal data:

  • There are no international data transfers
  • Your data remains in your jurisdiction at all times
  • No concerns regarding cross-border data flow apply
